Our Story

Who We Are

Ubumwe Donations is a community-driven organization dedicated to transforming lives across Rwanda through sustainable development initiatives and community support programs. Founded on the principle of "Ubumwe" (Unity in Kinyarwanda), we believe that collective action creates lasting change.

Since our inception, we have partnered with local communities, international organizations, and passionate individuals to address critical needs in education, healthcare, disaster relief, and poverty alleviation. Our approach combines grassroots understanding with strategic development to ensure every contribution makes a meaningful impact.

Our Mission

To unite communities and resources in creating sustainable solutions that empower vulnerable populations, promote social equity, and build resilient communities throughout Rwanda and beyond.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to basic necessities, opportunities for growth, and the support needed to thrive. We envision communities where unity drives progress and compassion guides action.
